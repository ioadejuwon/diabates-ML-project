How to Prevent Diabetes

Maintain a Healthy Weight

Keeping your weight within a healthy range can reduce your risk of diabetes. Aim for a balanced diet and regular exercise to achieve and maintain a healthy weight.

Monitor Your Blood Sugar Levels

If you have risk factors for diabetes, regular monitoring of your blood sugar levels can help you stay on top of your health and catch any early signs of diabetes.

Get Regular Check-ups

Regular health check-ups can help detect any early signs of diabetes. Your healthcare provider can provide personalized advice on how to prevent diabetes based on your health status and risk factors.
